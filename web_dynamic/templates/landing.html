Welcome to an online productivity app

About

Welcome to our Todo app, where organizing your tasks has never been easier or more efficient. We created our app to help people like you stay on top of their daily tasks and keep track of everything they need to do. Our app is designed to give you a clear and easy-to-use interface that lets you focus on your tasks without any distractions.

The app was built by Balogun Adedotun as a portfolio project for ALX Africa. You can find the app's code on my GitHub repository, github.com/Dotunoflagos/do_it.

Features

Our Todo app comes with a wide range of features that will make your task organization process quick and simple. Here are some of the key features:

Create Multiple Lists

With our app, you can create as many lists as you want and organize your tasks in each list.

Reminder

You can set up reminders for important tasks, so you never forget what needs to be done.

Important Tasks

This feature enables the user to label a task as important, and then the task is shifted to a unique smart list.

Easy-To-Use Interface

Our app has an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, making it simple to add, edit, and delete tasks.
