Welcome to Do-It an online productivity app

About

Welcome to our Todo app, where organizing your tasks has never been easier or more efficient. We created our app to help people like you stay on top of their daily tasks and keep track of everything they need to do. Our app is designed to give you a clear and easy-to-use interface that lets you focus on your tasks without any distractions.

The app was built by Balogun Adedotun as a portfolio project for ALX Africa. You can find the app's code on my GitHub repository, github.com/Dotunoflagos/do_it.
